Who We Are

Chieni Supermarket is a proudly Kenyan retail store dedicated to bringing quality, affordability, and convenience closer to our customers. Since our establishment, we have been committed to serving families and communities with a wide variety of products ranging from groceries, fresh produce, household essentials, to personal care and lifestyle items.

Our Mission

To make everyday shopping simple, affordable, and enjoyable while supporting local suppliers and creating a positive shopping experience for our customers.

Our Vision

To be the leading community-centered supermarket brand that customers can always rely on for quality products and exceptional service.
